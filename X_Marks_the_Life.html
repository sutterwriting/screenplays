X Marks the Life


 High school sucks. All who have endured it will agree. For many, romance is the only thing that gets them through these trying years. For Brian Marks, however, romance is what betrays him. As that cool guy that everybody wants to be, you’d expect that Brian could have any girl that he wants. That he could be happy. But nothing could be farther from the truth. With a dark past that haunts him, love seems to be Brian’s only escape but women are more complicated than Brian could ever have guessed.

 Kim, beautiful, talented, funny, and gay. Bree, perfect, popular, gorgeous, and suicidal. Adeline, mysterious, smart, lovely, and unfaithful. These are they that hold sway in Brian’s heart. These are they that could define or destroy him.
