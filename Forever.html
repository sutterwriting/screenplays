Forever


 A connection. A branch in the tracks. A fork in the road. A trail to the horizon. We all make choices every day. Choices that define who we will be. These choices create branches in time. Could a connection with another person be so strong that it transcends these branches to connect two people together?

 Brandon Kidd and Belle Hooper have such a connection. A connection that binds them together after a fire tries to tear them apart and slam time between them. Brandon must live three lives, two alone, in order to return to Belle and save everything that he cares about.

 They are forced together to survive after war shakes the foundation of the earth. In the small, and only, time they have together they forge a bond that time cannot break. A bond that connects them in other branches of time. Alas, this beautiful time together must come to an end. After an untimely death, Brad finds himself as an old man in a branch of time where Belle had been killed by fire as a girl.

 Brad must find his way back into a time where he and Belle can be together. As Brad goes from one branch to another trying to find Belle, he fights with the Greeks to take Troy, climbs Mt. Everest, sails with the Spanish Armada, and sees Egyptian mummies. All his travels come to two moments in time. The fire, and the start of the war.
